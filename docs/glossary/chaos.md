# Chaos

## Definition

Chaos is a structural signal that a chosen scope has lost admissibility or ordering dominance
over the degrees of freedom relevant to the intended application.

## Role in ARW

Chaos does not imply the absence of order.
It indicates scope mismatch: stabilized variables no longer suffice to describe the
system's effective behavior.

- Admissibility
- Scope Dominance
