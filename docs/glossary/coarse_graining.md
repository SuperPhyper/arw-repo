# Coarse-Graining

## Definition

Coarse-graining is the reduction of a fine-grained description to a higher-level one by
grouping many microstates into fewer macro-level structures.

## Role in ARW

Coarse-graining is closely related to observable reduction and partition formation.
Basins and emergent regimes often arise through such reduced descriptions.
