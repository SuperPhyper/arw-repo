# Emergent Solution Space

## Definition

An emergent solution space is the higher-level structured space of viable paths,
configurations, or behaviors that becomes visible when a new scope stabilizes.

## Role in ARW

At a lower scope, behavior may appear chaotic or unstable.

At a higher scope, the relevant object is no longer the individual trajectory but
the structured set of possible solutions.
