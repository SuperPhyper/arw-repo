# Latent Degrees of Freedom

## Definition

Latent degrees of freedom are variables that remain dynamically active even when
they are suppressed by the current descriptive scope.

## Role in ARW

Latent variables can accumulate influence over time and destabilize the current scope,
triggering scope transition.
