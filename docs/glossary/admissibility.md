# Admissibility

## Definition

Admissibility describes whether a descriptive scope remains structurally compatible
with the currently active degrees of freedom of a system.

## Role in ARW

A scope remains admissible as long as the degrees of freedom it suppresses remain
dynamically irrelevant under the system's boundary conditions.

Loss of admissibility leads to scope instability and may trigger scope transition.
