# Emergence

## Definition

Emergence describes the stabilization of new descriptive structures when a previous
scope loses admissibility and the effective state space reorganizes.

## Role in ARW

Emergence typically appears through scope transition.

Observable reduction and latent degrees of freedom reorganize the effective
partition of the system's state space.
