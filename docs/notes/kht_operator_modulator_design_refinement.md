# KHT Layer 1 — Operator/Modulator Design Refinement

**Scope.** A design-sharpening pass over the Layer 1 operator/modulator space
(`kht_architecture_layer1.md`), produced by turning ARW's own diagnostic patterns —
form/content separation, factorization, coverage, permutation symmetry, cover stability,
aggregation limits — onto KHT's own primitives. ART-level instantiation note. Nothing here
is confirmed; each section ends with one discriminating test. Items are status-tagged.

**Central results of this pass.**
1. The four operators are **GF(2)²** over two **operator-constituting** axes: **τ\* =
   context-aggregation depth (= S/N)** and **aperture = data class (referencing vs
   topology)**. The perception **duality** (Si–Ne / Se–Ni) is the **parity** — derived.
2. **Modulators are sequenced, not global-simultaneous.** The episode fixes the τ\*/aperture
   *profile* (the operator); modulators sequence within it; "global" character is an
   **aggregate**.
3. Discrete/continuum resolves **by level**: momentary discrete, aggregated continuous,
   **Type = requantization**. The requantization is **context-local winner-takes-all**;
   the global Type is the **profile of context-winners**, not a global winner or a centroid.
4. The **arm space** modulators traverse is **generated by branched flow** through a
   weakly-correlated medium during formative phases, then quenched. **V₄ = the symmetry of
   that medium.**

## 1. Two constituting axes: τ\* = S/N and aperture = data class

**[claim]** for the grid; **[hypothesis]** for both axes being the constituting basis.

The time *arrow* (directedness of ≤) is uniform across all (O, M) → folded into the
background. Two non-uniform, operator-constituting axes remain (changing either tips
operator identity):

- **τ\* = context-aggregation depth.** Deep aggregated context → the **N** operators;
  shallow → the **S** operators. **Low τ\* = Sensing, high τ\* = Intuition.** (Corrects the
  initial draft, which placed Si at high τ\* by confusing *reference store* with *context
  aggregation*. Si references a discrete concrete anchor — low aggregation — so Si is low τ\*.)
- **Aperture = data class.** Small aperture → **referencing** (Ni, Si); large → **topology**
  (Se, Ne).

Grid (τ\*-bit: N=1; aperture-bit: topology=1):

```
                 referencing (small)   topology (large)
τ* low  (S):     Si (0,0)              Se (0,1)
τ* high (N):     Ni (1,0)              Ne (1,1)
```

The four tile τ\* × aperture completely; two generators, no missing operators.

**Test (experiment-proposal).** Can τ\* and aperture each move *independently* — Si→Ni moves
τ\* alone, Si→Se moves aperture alone? Independent single-axis movement confirms two
orthogonal constituting knobs.

## 2. Operators as GF(2)²; the perception duality is the parity

**[claim]** — conditional on §1.

Parity = τ\* ⊕ aperture: Si=0, Se=1, Ni=1, Ne=0. The two parity classes **{Si, Ne}** and
**{Se, Ni}** are exactly the established perception axes Si–Ne and Se–Ni. So:

- **Generators (independent constituting knobs):** τ\* (= S/N) and aperture (= data class).
- **Derived (parity):** the perception **duality** Si–Ne / Se–Ni.

"A mode contains both a τ\* value and a data class" → the dual modes are Si–Ne and Se–Ni
(each spans both τ\* values and both apertures). The duality is **explained** as the parity
of the two generators, not postulated.

**[claim]**

On the corrected grid, the coverage-complete matching (each pair differs in **both**

## 4. The modulator layer is sequenced and predominantly discrete

**[hypothesis]**

### 4.1 Stable-output principle
A human processes neither a *null reference* nor a *double reference*: those corners produce
**no stable output**, so they are **forbidden** (cover-instability,
`docs/core/cover_stability_criterion.md`), not merely indistinguishable. The modulator binary
is forced by well-definedness, not a coarsening of a continuum. Per step, strictly two-valued.

### 4.2 I/E = Bezug (in/out), distinct from aperture (data class)
**[claim]** I/E gives the operator's **reference direction** (in/out) — the *Bezug*. Aperture
gives the **data class** — the *Datentyp*. Two different levels; MBTI's "introverted/
extraverted" uses one word for both. The Si-vs-Se contrast MBTI calls i/e is, in KHT, the
**aperture** (constituting); the genuine **I/E modulator** is the separate in/out Bezug
(modulating, metric-free, discrete — the "fixation radius" reading is withdrawn). Not
redundant with aperture.

### 4.3 T/F = permutation invariance
**[hypothesis]** T = criterion invariant under exchange of the parties; F = criterion breaks
that symmetry. A symmetry property, native ARW machinery, genuinely binary. Test: at
(Ne, TIP) vs (Ne, FIP), Te externally stabilized yet T, Fi internally stabilized yet F —
distinct → primitive; collapse → locus in disguise.

### 4.4 Orientation (past/future) — re-derivation pending
**[open-question]** Developed under the previous (corrected) grid; past/future may now be
partly derived from {τ\*, aperture}. Re-derive before asserting. → Q-KHT-OM-4.

### 4.5 Globality revised: episode fixes the profile, modulators sequence
**[claim]** — **revises** layer1 §2.1. The episode fixes the τ\*/aperture profile (the
operator); modulators vary **sequentially** within the episode. No momentary global modulator
state; "globality" emerges only as an aggregate (§5).

## 5. Discrete vs. continuum by level; Type as a context-local aggregation limit

**[interpretation]**

Level resolution of the discrete/continuum dispute:

- **Momentary** (a step): **discrete** — stable-output forbids double/null (§4.1).
- **Aggregated over time** (the sequence across episodes): **continuous** — usage frequency.
- **Type**: the **requantization** of the aggregate to either/or — a solidified usage profile.

So the binarized continuum exists **one level up** (aggregated frequency), not momentarily.
The Type is an **aggregation limit** (`arw_aggregation_limits_typological_observables.md` +
`arw_quantization_partition_stability.md`, stacked: sequence → frequency → requantized type).

**The requantization is winner-takes-all, but only context-locally.** Increasing aggregation
of experience drives **ever-stronger context-specific conditioning** → within a context the
dominant channel wins outright (WTA). So the local aggregation limit is **sharp** (the
brightest arm), with **no averaging** — hence **no phantom type** locally.

**The global Type is a distribution, not a winner.** A single global WTA collapse would be
**far too strong** (a person is not one channel). The global Type is therefore the **profile
of context-conditioned winners** — a map *context → dominant channel*, assembled — **neither**
a global WTA **nor** a centroid average. This **resolves the phantom-type risk**: the phantom
came from "average-then-requantize"; but requantization is per-context WTA (sharp), and the
global Type is the assembled pattern of local winners, not a centroid.

**Consequence for Layer 2's profile count.** A Type is an **aggregation-level** object
(a profile of context-winners over sequenced episodes), not a momentary operator ×
modulator-cluster product. Layer 2's 16/32 counting must be reinterpreted accordingly; this
reframes — and may dissolve — the earlier "16 vs 32 cluster-count collision".

## 6. Generation and selection of the arm space

**[hypothesis]** — strong claims; falsifiers in §6.4 make them mechanism, not analogy.

Two separable questions: what **generates** the arm space, and what **selects** the arms
traversed. §5 answers selection (context-local WTA conditioning). This section answers
generation.

### 6.1 Generation by branched flow
Biological cognition: the arm space is a **branched-flow** pattern through a **weakly-
correlated random medium** of aggregated contexts. Branched flow produces rich branching
topology from *weak* correlations via cumulative weak focusing — so the genetic **Restriction**
BC need not encode the arms; it need only set the **correlation structure of the medium**, and
the branching dynamics generate the topology emergently. **Restriction reframed:** from
"seed weighting" to "medium correlation"; arms are *emergent*, not inscribed. **Randomized
activation during formative/critical phases** realizes the branched networks, which
developmental Hebbian consolidation then **quenches** into synaptic topology.

### 6.2 Two epochs / two timescales
- **Generation:** branched flow during formative phases, **quenched** (frozen), not merely slow.
- **Selection:** lifelong reweighting (context-local WTA, §5) over the **frozen** arm space.

Adiabatic separation is secured by **developmental timing** — the arm space freezes after the
critical period, so selection cannot perturb it fast enough to collapse the levels. This
resolves the earlier adiabaticity worry (the separation is a timing fact, not a running rate
condition). **Note — two-epoch Dissipation:** Hebbian consolidation appears twice (freezing in
development; ongoing reweighting after). Same BC, two roles — must be named separately or they
drift (→ §7).

### 6.3 V₄ as the symmetry of the medium; idiographic/nomothetic resolution
V₄ survives **not** as a transition law but as the **symmetry of the medium** — the GF(2)²
structure on {τ\*, aperture} plus duality (§2). Branched flow over a symmetric medium inherits
the symmetry in its **branch statistics**. Selection (Restriction-seed + Dissipation-
consolidation) then needs **no separate governing law**.

This dissolves the idiographic/nomothetic tension: branched flow is **individually seed-
dependent but statistically universal**. The **concrete** arm topology of a person is seed-
contingent and personal (idiographic, non-reproducible); the **Type categories** are the
**medium statistics** (nomothetic, universal). The Type lives in the medium invariants, not in
the individual branch pattern — which is why Types are stable across individuals while no two
trajectories are identical.

### 6.4 Falsifiers (mechanism, not analogy) — **[experiment-proposal]**
- **Branching scale.** Branched flow has a characteristic distance to first caustic, scaling
  with medium strength by a power law. → There should be a **typical context-depth at which
  arms first bifurcate**, not an arbitrary one.
- **Branched → diffusive crossover.** Beyond many correlation lengths, branch structure washes
  into speckle. → **Very deep aggregated contexts (high τ\*) should lose clean arm structure
  and go diffuse**; arm clarity is **non-monotone in τ\***, peaking at the branching scale.

Claim these signatures → the mechanism is falsifiable. Decline them → it is only an analogy and
should not be leaned on.

## 7. Open consistency debt

- **Two-epoch Dissipation naming.** Hebbian consolidation as developmental *freezing* (§6.2)
  vs. lifelong *reweighting* (§5) are one BC in two roles. Name them distinctly in the layer2
  BC doc or they drift into one another.
- **Cluster count → reframed.** With aperture constituting and the Type redefined as an
  aggregation-level object (§5), the old "16 vs 32" framing is wrong; layer2's duality map and
  profile derivation (built on a momentary product and on J/P as a modulator) need rederivation.
- **Coupling in (potentially) three places.** Se (operator), Ego/Shadow (Layer 2 emergent),
  and — only if T/F is read as coupling — the modulator. Permutation-invariance (§4.3) avoids
  this; keep it.
- **External substrate coverage unproven.** Layer 2's internal coverage criterion is Lemma-G-
  type; it does not establish operator coverage of all ARW substrate forms (Axiom-A-type).
- **Q-REL-04 link.** KHT separates fiber-generation (Ne, topology) from dissipation (BC); the
  arw-repo Dissipation primitive conflates them (fixed fiber dimension). Branched flow sharpens
  this: does Ne branch in a fixed topology, or grow dimension (genuine branching)?
